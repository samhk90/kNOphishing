What is Phishing?

Phishing is an attack that attempts to steal your identity, by getting you to reveal personal information; such as credit card numbers, bank information or passwords, on websites that pretend to be legitimate. Attackers pretend to be reputable companies, friends, in a fake message, which contains a link to a phishing website.
